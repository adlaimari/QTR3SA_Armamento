About Us

To empower and build a strong community for LGBT people.

Why we do this?

With alot of LGBT people being discriminated and feeling left out of our society. We aim to build a place where each and every member of the LGBT community has freedom to live their truth without fear and build their characters to face the world and fight for the equality they deserve.

 

What we offer?

A safe haven for the members of the LGBT community where everyone is welcomed for who they are. We offer advocacy, health and wellness programs to help you.
