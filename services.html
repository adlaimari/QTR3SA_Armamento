Our Services

We offer advocacy, health and wellness programs, entertainment and cultural events.

Advocacy

The Rainbow Center is a place for LGBT community organizing and innovation, a key to a progressive social change and will provide critical services for the community. We will provide seminars and programs that will help create leaders for the community. Together we fight for:

  • Equity and Inclusion for Transgender & Gender Nonconforming to the Society
  • LGBT Visibility & Affirmation
  • Justice
 

Entertainment and Cultural Events

The Rainbow Center will be a cultural center for the LGBT community, a place to showcase original work by artist of the LGBT community and will offer a wide variety of arts and entertainment facilities like:

  • Annual Events to celebrate Pride for being who you are.
  • Venue for events
  • Sports facilities i.e. badminton courts or basketball courts
 

Health and Wellness Programs

The Rainbow Center will be a safe haven for the members of the LGBT community that is suffering from abuse and discrimination. We will provide programs designed for the LGBT community to help these members to address problems like:

  • Substance or Alcohol addiction
  • Depression and Anxiety
